About Our School

Welcome to New Milton public School

New Milton Public School is a modern junior division co-educated English medium non-aided school, COMMENCED in 1996 by Late shri Rajkumar Kulshrestha ‘Director of Milton Public School, Agra’ for his brother Mr. K. K. Singh ‘Manager of New Milton Public School, Agra’ to provide a sound academic, religious and moral education to all student regardless of cast ,creed ,religion ,physically and mentally fit to become active useful and worthy citizen of India.
The school has been set up with the vision of providing the best educational facilities to its students. The students will be provided with an inter-disciplinary education, by exposing them to extra curricular and vocational courses. A sense of social responsibility is developed, thereby making every student of NMPS a role-model for society.

MISSION STATEMENT
The aim of NMPS is to enhance individual growth so as to form a part of the world community. This we believe is possible as NMPS offers innovative educational tools and inspires the minds of the pupils to think creatively within the established frame work.
NMPS also promotes an all round physical development of the student by active participation in sports and extra curricular activities. By having student from diverse backgrounds, NMPS is uniquely positioned to integrate cultural diversity in the school, so that the students share the uniqueness of our culture.

ADMISSION
  1. Children are admitted normally from play group ,L.K.G,U.K.G ,1 st standards in month of april and in special case in the month of july.
  2. Admission of classes from II TO VIII shall be subject to availability of seats
  3. Admission will be based on test and parents interviews .
  4. The principal reserves the right to grant admission or otherwise to student without having to assign reason for her action
  5. Fee once charged shall not be returned
  6. Session will be started from 2 nd april to 31 st march of next year
FACILITIES

Children who are studying In our school,after passing out from class VIII can directly move to MILTON PUBLIC SCHOOL which is affiliated to ALL india senior school certificate examination of new delhi to complete their X and XII board examination without any ADMISSION TEST
